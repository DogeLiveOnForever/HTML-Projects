About Guinea Pigs

The guinea pig, also known as cavy, is a species of rodent belonging to the family Caviidae and the genus Cavia.

Despite their name, guinea pigs are not from Guinea, althrough many people think that.

Unlike other rodents, guinea pigs make great pets for younger children. Why? You may ask.

Hamsters, gerbals, etc. are very squirmy and bite (or at least a lot more that guinea pigs), also more hard

to take care of. Guinea pigs are gental and calm. Thy are also very easy to take care of.
